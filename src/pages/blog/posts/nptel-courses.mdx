The National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning (NPTEL) is an MHRD initiative established in 2003 by seven IITs and IISc Bangalore to provide open-access online lectures in core fields like engineering and sciences.

NPTEL hosts the world’s largest repository of online courses in engineering, sciences, and select humanities subjects, with over 56,000 hours of video content and more than 470 million views. Since 2014, it has offered open online courses with optional certificates from the IITs/IISc, available upon passing an examination.

---

## NPTEL at IIT Patna

Students at IIT Patna can utilize NPTEL courses by seeking approval from the Department Academic Program Committee. Upon successful completion, credits and grades are transferred to their transcript.

- **Credit Limitations**: Earn up to 8 credits, equivalent to 16 credits in the old curriculum and 8 in the 2023 curriculum onwards.
- **Elective Fulfillment**: Can fulfill up to 2 department elective requirements.

---

## How to Get Started with NPTEL

1. **Registration**: Visit the [NPTEL Form and Guidelines](https://academics.iitp.ac.in/files/NPTEL_Guidelines.pdf) and register for courses under the ‘NOC’ section.
2. **Course Selection**: Choose courses relevant to your discipline. Check if they align with the electives listed in your department.
3. **Seek Approval**: Submit an application to the Department Academic Program Committee for credit consideration.
4. **Course Completion**: Attend lectures, submit assignments, and pass the final exam to earn a certificate.
5. **Grade Transfer**: After course completion, submit your certificate for credit and grade transfer.
